Our History & Mission

Our Humble Beginnings

The Redeemed Gospel Church was founded in 1979 by Bishop Arthur Kitonga, who began with a vision to reach the lost and transform communities through the power of the Gospel. What started as a small fellowship in Nairobi, Kenya, quickly grew into a dynamic movement as people responded to the message of hope and redemption.

Rapid Growth & Expansion

In the early years, the church emphasized evangelism, discipleship, and nurturing believers in their faith journey. The movement expanded rapidly across Kenya and beyond, establishing churches in urban centers and rural areas alike, always maintaining its commitment to biblical teaching and practical ministry.

Redeemed Gospel Church Karatina

Redeemed Gospel Church Karatina was planted as part of this growing network, bringing the same life-changing message to the people of Karatina and surrounding communities. Under the leadership of our dedicated pastors, we continue the original vision of our founders - to see lives transformed by the power of Jesus Christ.

Our Mission Today

Today, Redeemed Gospel Church stands as one of Kenya's largest evangelical movements, with thousands of churches across the nation and international presence, all united in the mission to "know Christ and make Him known." Our mission is to proclaim the Gospel of Jesus Christ, disciple believers, and demonstrate God's love through compassionate ministry to all people.
